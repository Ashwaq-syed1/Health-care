Clinical Researcher on a Mission

Meet Dr. Maya, a Clinical Researcher. Maya works in a fast-paced hospital environment where every second counts. She needs quick, reliable access to medical knowledge—disease definitions, diagnostic protocols, treatment guidelines—without flipping through hundreds of pages or scattered PDFs.

Fragmented Knowledge, Slower Care

Today, accessing medical knowledge is cumbersome. Doctors and researchers rely on fragmented sources—manual searches in PDFs, outdated portals, and keyword-based systems that fail to capture context. This slows down diagnosis, increases cognitive load, and impacts patient care.

Clinical Intelligence, powered by RAG and AlloyDB

Introducing the AI-Powered Clinical Knowledge Assistant—a RAG-based application built for healthcare professionals. It combines structured medical data with unstructured content from comprehensive medical encyclopedias, enabling natural language queries and delivering accurate, context-rich answers instantly.



The Journey of Emma

Now, let’s step into Dr. Maya’s world. Her journey is effortless: she types ‘What are the diagnostic tests for Neutropenia disease?’—and AI does the rest. Behind the scenes, the system retrieves relevant sections from the medical encyclopedia, combines them with structured data, and summarizes the answer. Maya gets precise, actionable insights in seconds.
